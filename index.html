Scout

Scout is a 2D platform-based game where the protagonist, Scout, journeys through the land in search of their home. The player controls Scout through 3 levels, each with their own unique time and setting to help Scout. The player will encounter others, have to make decisions, and learn what it means to have a home. Scout is controlled by the left and right arrow keys along with the spacebar on the keyboard. The player's task to get Scout through the level and avoid the various obstacles. At end of each level, scout will have a chance to choose between different characters to bring along on their journey for a home.

Responsibilities

  • Scene Management
  • UI Creation

Combat Evolved

A project for CS 175 Project in AI class. We trained an AI based in Microsoft's Malmo and Minecraft using a neural network we developed using the Tensorflow API and Reinforced Deep Q-Learning. We passed in data such as position of enemies relative to the AI, its line of sight, and also images for it recognize when an enemy was in front of it. We rewarded the AI for landing attacks or killing a target while taking away reward points for taking damage and ultimately dying. The project was successful in teaching the AI to fight against a range of 1-3 Zombies with average win-rates of above 66.5% when using the strongest weapon while having a 40% win-rate when facing 1v1 with the weakest weapon.

Responsibilities

  • Neural Network Development
  • Organizing Team Meetings
  • Planning tests and scenarios

Orchestra Simulator

An Orchestra Simulator based with the player as a manager of an Orchestra. As the manager, the player will schedule auditions, plan recitals and rehearsals, and schedule other events. The player will work from their apartment, schedule auditions and practices at the practice hall, and play at large venues within our city. The player's performance will then be measured by critics who will assign them a score based on how well their orchestra performed. With little practice, the orchestra will fail to perform their piece well and result in off-beat tempos and unsynchronized performers. Listen to how your orchestra will perform.

Responsibilities

  • Scene Management
  • Character Movement (Auto-Pathing)
  • Orchestra Management
  • UI Creation

Ninja Story

Ninja Story is a 2D platformer action RPG that I made for ICS 161 as my second project by myself. This game focuses heavily on 2D colliders with damage detection and precise timing of actions. Our hero finds themselves awake one day in a world filled with magic and must defeat his enemies to return home. The world is filled with knights and dragons that will do whatever they can to defeat the ninja for trespassing onto their land. With each passing portal, enemies become stronger and the ninja must quickly dodge his enemy's attacks and avoid losing life as there will be no restoration potions in this world. Assets were obtained from the Assets store.

Responsibilities

  • Scene Management
  • Gameplay (Player Controls)
  • Enemy AI
  • Weapon Colliders
  • UI Creation
